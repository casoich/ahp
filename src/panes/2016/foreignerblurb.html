Filled with humor, romance, drama and nonstop melody this musical is a thrilling celebration of life and the spirit that keeps us all going. Enjoy the musical genius of Kander & Ebb songs: “All that Jazz,” “Class,” “The Rink,” “Kiss of the Spider Woman,” “Marry Me,” “We Can Make It,” “Cabaret”, “ New York, New York,” “Mr. Cellophane,” and many, many others.
