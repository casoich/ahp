Theatre Camp at Apple Hill Playhouse - 2017

LET’S MAKE A SHOW!

This class uses an age-appropriate musical script to develop skills in acting, singing, and dancing, meshing those individual skills into an ensemble performance that emphasizes responsibility, reliability, commitment, and cooperation. Students are responsible for providing/creating their own costumes, properties, programs and set pieces, using materials here at the Playhouse or brought from home.

Camps are held at the Delmont American Legion, 54 Freeport St., Delmont, PA

Performance for invited audience on stage at Apple Hill Playhouse. FREE TO ALL (donations welcome.)

Ages 6-12 Tuition $175.00 $50 non refundable deposit required with registration.

Each session has it's own script.

Full tuition with registration $150. Save $25.

Session One: Two week program: 9:30 – 12:30 Mon-Fri June 19 –June 30 Performance July 1 at 11AM

Session Two: Two week program: 9:30 – 12:30 Mon-Fri July 17-28 Performance July 29 at 11AM

Individual coaching for older students by arrangement.
