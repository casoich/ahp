Theatre Camp at Apple Hill Playhouse - 2016

LET’S MAKE A SHOW!

This class uses an age-appropriate musical script to develop skills in acting, singing, and dancing, meshing those individual skills into an ensemble performance that emphasizes responsibility, reliability, commitment, and cooperation. Students are responsible for providing/creating their own costumes, properties, programs and set pieces, using materials here at the Playhouse or brought from home.

Performance for invited audience on stage at Apple Hill Playhouse. FREE TO ALL (donations welcome.)
