A very special Friend-and-Relation tells Christopher Robin a story of a day long ago (about last year) when Eeyore, the old gray donkey who lives by himself in the thistle corner of Hundred Acre Wood, was absolutely miserable. He had lost his tail and Winnie-the-Pooh and Piglet got all of their friends and a search was organized. There is a great deal of fun and excitement, plus delightful songs, in the adventure that follows. Its theme, which concerns caring, sharing and the importance of cultivating friends, makes this a very special and enduring musical for all.
