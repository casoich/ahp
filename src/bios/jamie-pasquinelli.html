Jamie is excited to begin her debut on Apple Hill’s stage in The World Goes Round! She is currently working as a junior at Seton Hill University to achieve two bachelor degrees in Musical Theatre and Communication. Some of her most memorable shows include Guys and Dolls, Beauty and the Beast, Get Smart, Seven Brides for Seven Brothers, Power Lunch, and A Little Night Music. From working in the director’s chair, to backstage, to performing on stage, Jamie’s passion is to show the world the joy of theatre. She would like to thank the cast, crew, her family, and especially her crazy stage mom for giving her the opportunity to preform in this fantastic show! Enjoy!
