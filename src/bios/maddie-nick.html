Maddie is super excited to be performing in her first show at Apple Hill Playhouse. She is currently a student at Pitt-Johnstown and is studying Business Management, Marketing, and IS. Maddie has performed in The Theatre Factory's Chicago and Children of Eden. Maddie has also performed in Penn-Trafford Drama Guild's Annie Get Your Gun, Oliver, The Hobbit, and Guys and Dolls. She has also performed in a number of productions at Stage Right! School for the performing arts such as RENT, Miss Saigon, Hairspray, and FAME. She spends her free time volunteering for the Penn Middle School Drama Club every year and just recently started a Musical Theatre club at her school. Maddie would like to thank everyone involved with this experience and looks forward to working at the Apple Hill Playhouse again.
