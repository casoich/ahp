Katelynn Reist has recently graduated from Seton Hill University with a Bachelor of Arts is Musical Theatre/Dance. Born and raised in Eastern PA, she is excited to become a part of the Apple Hill Playhouse family. She has done scene work and internships with the Pittsburgh Public Theatre and the City Theatre Company in Pittsburgh. She has done previous stage management work with the No Name Players. She thanks the cast and crew for making her Apple Hill debut exciting and for all of the hard work they put into this production.
