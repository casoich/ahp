Matthew R. Mlynarski (Director) is excited to be back working with his friends at Apple Hill! He has a BA in Theatre Performance from Seton Hill University in Greensburg, PA where he focused his studies on acting and directing. Some of his most memorable shows include Pterodactyls, Bright Ideas, Wonder Of The World, Dog Sees God..., And Avenue Q. Matt is currently working on being an actor and director in the Pittsburgh area. He would like to thank the cast and crew for a great run.
