Stephen is delighted to be returning to Apple Hill for his second season, especially with this talented cast and crew. Recent credits include Ken in Rumors at Greensburg Civic Theatre, It’s a Wonderful Life: A Live Radio Play at The Theatre Factory, Banquo in Macbeth and David in A Piece of Cake at South Park Theatre. Stephen also made his film debut last summer in Dangerwood Picture’s TV Pilot Mason as well as their 2014 entry in the Pittsburgh 48 Hour Film Festival. When not acting, Stephen splits time between working as a pastry chef, chasing his dog Gizmo and renovating his house.
