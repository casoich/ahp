Patrick is happy to be making his Apple Hill debut in The World Goes 'Round. He recently graduated from Seton Hill University with a BFA in Musical Theatre. Past roles include Bobby Strong in Urinetown the Musical, The Son in Six Characters in Search of an Author, Tartuffe in Tartuffe, and Fredrik Egerman in A Little Night Music - all at Seton Hill University. Patrick would like to thank Denise, Michael, Surya, and the incredibly talented cast for a fun and rewarding experience.
