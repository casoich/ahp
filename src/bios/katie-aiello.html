Katie is thrilled to be back on stage at Apple Hill! After taking time off to have two children, she's excited to be performing again. Most recently appearing as Sally Bowles in The Split Stage Production of Cabaret. This is not her first time on Apple Hill's stage. Aside from spending her summers here growing up she appeared in The Taffeta's and Why Do Fools Fall In Love. Katie is thrilled to be reprising her role as Patsy under the direction of her sister! She first performed as Patsy in the Theater Factory's production of Always, Patsy Cline. Katie has performed in various theaters. Some other favorite credits include, Hodel in Fiddler on the Roof, Electra in Gypsy, and Gladys in The Pajama Game. She is also the owner of Bella Amici Salon in Irwin. She would like to thank her family for their continued support in helping her follow her passion in the theater.
