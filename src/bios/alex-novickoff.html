Alex is happy to be debuting at Apple Hill in this show. Alex has appeared with the New Ken Civic Theatre in Leading Ladies, Wonder of the World, On Golden Pond, Charlotte's Web and Proposals. Alex is a fourth grade teacher in the New Kensington-Arnold School District.
