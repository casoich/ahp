Stephen is excited and humbled to be back in the director’s chair after many, many years absent. He studied Theatre at the College of Charleston in South Carolina. Since moving to Pittsburgh, he has worked with numerous theatre companies in the area including Old School House Players, The Theatre Factory, New Kensington Civic Theatre, Apple Hill Playhouse, Poor Yorick’s Players, Greensburg Civic Theatre and South Park Theatre. Stephen would like to thank Pat for giving him the opportunity in bringing this great show to life. Thanks also to this unbelievable cast for all their hard work and amazing talent.
