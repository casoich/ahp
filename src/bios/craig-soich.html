Craig is excited to return to the stage this season at Apple Hill Playhouse! In addition to serving on the board of AHP, he is also active with Valley Players of Ligonier and Greensburg Civic Theatre. Last season at Apple Hill Playhouse, he portrayed Bernard S. Dunlap in Suite Surrender, and Count Puchlik of Puszczykowo in Secondary Cause of Death, and has just finished playing Freddie in Noises Off in Ligonier. Thanks go to all his castmates for accepting him late into the fold, so to speak. He dedicates whatever happens onstage to his children Alec, Kaley, Camden, and Avery, as well as the rest of his crazy-sideways-extended family.
