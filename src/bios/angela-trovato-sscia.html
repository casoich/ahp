Angela Trovato is a graduate of the School of Theatre and Dance at James Madison University. While an undergraduate she directed Diana Son’s Stop Kiss and performed in several shows, her favorite role being Dunyasha in Chekhov’s The Cherry Orchard. She recently played Nina in New Kensington Civic Theatre’s production of Vanya and Sonia and Masha and Spike, and Suzanne/The Countess in Picasso at the Lapin Agile for the inaugural production at Hambone’s Dinner Theatre in Lawrenceville. Angela is excited to be on stage at Apple Hill for the first time and would like to thank her family for their love and support.
