At age five, Mike Marra starred as the Candy Man in a kindergarten choir concert at Glade View elementary school, now a condominium in Lower Burrell. "The essence of my role was to pass out candy, a swirled taffy," he recalls. "My mom and grandma were sitting way in the back, but the song ended, and I had not yet reached them. So I just kept passing out taffy until I did, to the applause and laughter of the crowd. I got a great big kiss, too." Since then, Mike has been largely typecast in similar roles. When he's not designing sound or scenery, stage managing, building sets, or acting for Apple Hill, Mike can usually be found in the parking lot throwing a boomerang or contemplating divine mysteries.
