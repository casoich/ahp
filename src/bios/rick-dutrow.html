RICK DUTROW (Mortimer), we are excited to announce, will appear in 2 more shows this season, next in The Fox On The Fairway, and then in The Outgoing Tide to close out our great 2015 season! Rick was voted as 2014 Favorite Actor by the audiences at Apple Hill Playhouse. Also, in 2007 Rick was awarded the "Best Lead Performance" by The Pittsburgh Post-Gazette for his portrayal of Rick Stedman in AHP’s The Nerd. Rick recently delighted our audiences as his unforgettable Inspector Pratt in installment 1 & 2 of the Pratt Trilogies, first in Murdered to Death, 2013 and then in the sequel, Secondary Cause of Death, 2014. Rick also appeared with us as Mr. Pippet in Suite Surrender, 2014. His AHP history of roles includes: John Smith (Caught in the Net, 2013), Dr. Hercule Molineaux (The Ladies Man, 2011), and Starbuck (The Rainmaker, 2009). Rick has starred in AHP's productions of Cat on a Hot Tin Roof, Something to Hide, Misery, One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest, and Dial "M” for Murder. Rick was Henry Perkins in Funny Money 2003 (Theatre Factory). He has performed in numerous stage productions, commercials, industrial films, and made-for-TV movies, filmed around the Pittsburgh area for the past 30+ years. He was a Theatrical Performance Major and studied at St Vincent College. Currently a real estate agent with Howard Hanna Real Estate, and ranked in the top 5% in the USA. He has 3 children he loves even more than theatre.
